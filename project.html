Check-In

Standard check-in time is 12pm - 6pm. Please inform hotels.ca if you'll be checking in later than 5pm to ensure your reservation is not cancelled by the hotel.


Refunds & Cancellation

Hotels are settled 24- 48 hours before your check in date. Please endeavour to call or email to cancel before this time. Refunds on cancellations after hotel has been paid may take 3 working days or longer depending on the hotel's policy. Refunds cannot be made on any reservations made on a non-refundable room rate.
